NIN Registration


EarthTab offers essential services to help individuals and organizations register for the National Identification Number (NIN), a unique identifier required for various government and financial services in Nigeria.
Key Services Provided:
NIN Enrollment: Facilitating the collection of biometric data (fingerprints, facial recognition) and personal information for NIN registration.

Verification: Checking the authenticity of NINs to ensure accurate and valid registration records.

Data Correction: Assisting individuals in correcting any errors in their NIN records, such as incorrect names, addresses, or other personal details.

NIN Retrieval: Helping individuals retrieve their NIN if lost or forgotten, by accessing the national database.

Corporate NIN Registration: Assisting organizations in registering employees, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements for staff identification.
